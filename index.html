TOURIST PLACES IN KARNATAKA

TOP FIVE DESTINATIONS AND PLACES TO VISIT

  1. Bangalore – The City of Diverse Existence

    Topping the list for the right reasons in the capital city, Bangalore.Bangalore is the
    most cosmopolitan city in India.

  2. Bandipur National Park – In Harmony with Nature

    Get as close as you can to natural wildlife in Bandipur National Park.Perfect haven
    for animal lovers who want to get engrossed in the sights and sounds of the forest.

  3. Hampi – An Architectural Marvel

    Hampi is one of the world’s heritage sites as declared by UNESCO.Situated on the
    banks of Tungabhadra, the place is home to historical ruins and remains of the
    Vijayanagar kingdom.

  4. Gokarna – Tranquility at its Best

    Gokarna, a small and less populated town is situated in the north Kannad district of
    Karnataka. This is normally on the “to-go” places list of tourists due to its peaceful
    ambience.

  5. Mysore – City of Old World Charm

    Mysore is one of the oldest cities and second biggest in Karnataka. It was the erstwhile city of the Mysore kings who ruled the state for 100’s of years up until the 20th century.
